Wo·o·o·ordle

  • Welcome to Wo·o·o·ordle

    Similar to the popular game Wordle, in Wo·o·o·ordle you are trying to guess the 4-letter word. You have a total of 6 attempts and if you do not guess the word by the 6th attempt, you lose. If you guess a word and the letter is in the same spot in the original word as it is on the grid, the grid will turn green. If the word contains the letter but not in that spot, the grid will turn orange. If the letter is not in the word, the grid will stay grey. You can click the 'Start Over' button to reset your guesses and the word. Good luck Wo·o·o·ordle'ing!
